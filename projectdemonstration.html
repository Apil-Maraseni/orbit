About

The Project Demonstration event is designed to showcase innovative engineering and architectural projects that have real-world applications. Participants will present their projects, explaining their design, functionality, and potential impact.

Structure

1.Activity: Participants demonstrate an original project.

2. Team Size: 1-4 participants.

3. Project Type: Engineering or architecture-related projects.

4. Submission: A detailed presentation, supported by a prototype or model.

5. Judging Criteri Relevancy to the Topic, Impact of model, Uniqueness and Demonstration Technique.

6. Size of Model: Participants should pre-inform organizing committee about their model size (about 1 week before).

Timeline

> Registration Opens: 2 months before the event.

> Registration Closes: 1 week before the event.

> Submission Deadline: 1 week before the event.

> Event Date: December 6,7 and 8, 2024.

FAQs

FAQs:
1. Can the project be a continuation of a previous work?
Yes, as long as the innovation is highlighted.

2. Can we present digital models?
Yes, digital or physical models are acceptable.

3. How much time will each team have to present their project?
Teams will have 10-15 minutes to present, including time for questions from judges

Rules

1. Projects must be original or contain substantial modifications.

2. Presentation is limited to 10 minutes.

3. All team members should participate in the presentation.
